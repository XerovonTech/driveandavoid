How to play

  • Use LEFT / RIGHT buttons or arrow keys to change lanes
  • Tap SHOOT, or spacebar to fire a triple‑bullet burst and destroy enemy cars
  • Avoid enemy cars and the black orb's projectiles
  • Reach the distance goal to complete each level (20 total)
  • You have 3 lives; each life starts with 100% health
